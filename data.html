DATA & INSIGHTS

This page presents the evidence behind the issue; combining global statistics, government data, and local community surveys to understand how e‑waste is currently managed.

Global E‑Waste Management

Globally, less than a quarter of electronic waste is formally recycled. The majority is dumped, stored, or processed by the informal sector, causing environmental and health hazards.

Community Survey - Bangalore

Most respondents indicated that unused electronic devices are stored at home, not discarded. This reveals a major opportunity for reuse and organised recycling.

What This Data Tells Us

People are willing to recycle, but systems are inconvenient or unclear.
E‑waste is not destroyed, it accumulates slowly.
With proper collection networks, recovery rates can increase dramatically.
